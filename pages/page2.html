Dr. Marian Diamond

11.11.1926 - 25.07.2017

Who is Dr. Marian Diamond?

  • She was a pioneer in the development of modern Neuroscience
  • She is famous for her studies into Einstein's brain
  • Her work into the brain's ability to adapt and change gave rise to the modern concept known as 'Neuroplasticity'
  • Marian also spent many years of her career as an educator of Neuroatamoy at Cornell and Berkley
